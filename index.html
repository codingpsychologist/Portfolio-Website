About Me

Thank you for visiting my portfolio website. I am excited to share my most recent projects with you. I was born and raised in Greenville, MS. I attended high school at Brewer High School in Fort Worth, TX. I studied Psychology at Oral Roberts University in Tulsa, OK and graduated with a masters degree and doctoral degree in school psychology from Howard University in Washington, DC. I started coding in 2018 out of curiosity, developed a sincere interest in creating things programmatically, and have not looked back since. My first language was Python. I took a course on Udemy and countless YouTube tutorials, and I really enjoy learning the language. I am currently completing Free Code Camp, where I am receiving the structured training I need, and I dedicate at least one hour per day coding. I am in the process of launching my online community, Coding Psychologist. The goal of the platform is to teach my former colleagues code, while sharing my journey. I believe it's the best way to truly learn concepts. I am excited about my new journey in web development.
